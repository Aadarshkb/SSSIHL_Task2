# Binary Breakdown of Instructions

## 1. `beqz a5, 1017c`
**Hex:** `0x02078863`  
**Binary:** `0000 0010 0000 0111 1000 1000 0110 0011`  
**Breakdown:**  
- Immediate: `0000 0010 000` (split for B-type)  
- RS1: `00111` (a5)  
- RS2: `00000`  
- Funct3: `000`  
- Opcode: `1100011`
